The Artist Behind The Glow

Hi, I'm Saritha — Your Glow Expert

With over 5 years of dedicated artistry, I've built my craft around one philosophy: makeup should feel like a flawless second skin, never a mask. Every face I touch tells a unique story, and I'm here to make yours unforgettable.

My specialization in color theory and cream blending allows me to create looks that photograph beautifully in any light — whether you're a fair complexion or a radiant dusky tone (which I absolutely love working with). I continuously upskill under industry masters to ensure every technique I use is current, precise, and perfected.

Based in Madurai & Coimbatore, I specialize in South Indian Muhurtham Bridal looks, Engagements, Baby Showers, and creative editorial styles. Open to traveling worldwide.
